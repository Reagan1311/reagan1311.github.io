Hi! I am currently a third-year PhD student at School of Informatics in the University of Edinburgh, fully funded by [CDT-RAS](https://www.edinburgh-robotics.org/) scholarship.
My supervisors are Dr. [Laura Sevilla](https://laurasevilla.me/) and Prof. [Timothy Hospedales](https://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/thospeda/), 
and I also work closely with Dr. [Varun Jampani](https://varunjampani.github.io/) and Dr. [Deqing Sun](https://deqings.github.io/) at Google research. 

<!-- Before Edinburgh, I received my Master's degree from Sungkyunkwan University in 2020, and the Bachelor's degree from Xidian University in 2018. -->

My research interests lie in few-shot learning, learning under limited supervision, and multi-modal deep learning. 
Currently, I am working on visual affordance and object functionality understanding.

:loudspeaker: I am actively seeking a <u>research intern</u> position this year! Please feel free to contact me if you have any available positions or potential collaborations.
